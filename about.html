About Us

    About Oosrand SOS

    Our school, OOSRAND SECONDARY, is a no-fee Public School, situated in the East Rand township in Reiger Park of Gauteng's Ekurhuleni region. Its no-fee status is as a result of the compelling conditions of its service community and by extension its learner body. Conditions of high-unemployment, low-to-middle family income levels, critical mass of child-headed homes, rampant instances of substance abuse among adult and sadly, child family members are some of the socio-economic challenges faced by residents and the community at large. Transitioning from a conventional Secondary School to a School of specialisation in Entrepreneurship and Commerce, with a focus on Hospitality and Tourism; grants our learners and the greater community the skills required to generate businesses, be self-sufficient and academically equip for the working world that awaits them.

MORE ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Our school is created to address the skills shortage in our country by leveraging partnerships between industry players and schools.

OosRand Commerce and Entrepreneurship school of specialisation with the focus on Hospitality and Tourism strives to be a fully ICT functional academic institution: Learners are taught with the use of Smartboards, tablets, laptops and innovative technological lessons, presented by our most valuable resource; our dynamic educator body. As an ICT compliant school this grants us the allowance for online applications, online food purchases and e-commerce opportunities. Keeping our learner body, staff and community updated and connected to the world.
