History

THE SCHOOLS HISTORY

The name of the school comes from it's geographical location-eastern part of the Reef (mining zone). The school was formed in 1967 as a school for "colored people" under the Apartheid laws Being the only coloured high school on the East Rand at the time, it serviced communities from as far as Nigel, Eden Park,Germiston (Dukathole) and Geluksdal whose learners commuted by bus. This is the third building structure the school occupies,having started at what is now Drommedaris primary, then relocated to an Asbestos structure adjacent to this more recent structure in 1979. Mr Arendse is the 6th principle of the school since it's inception in 1967. Over it's 47 years,the school successfully transitioned into a fully integrated school, in it's composition of staff and learners.

It has always been a school catering for education needs of less affluent needy community Reiger Park with it's informal settlements, is known as. Being a school of specialisation is one of the best things that has happened to the school. The name of the school is purely geographical because we situated on the east rand of the gauteng province.At that time it was the trasval province The name indicates the location in the province. Oosrand is translated from english to afrikaans for eastrand highschool.The reason why they gave it that name is because it catered for a lot of locations on the eastrand it didnt only at the time cater for only Reiger Park we had learners come in from far places, because it was the only coloured school at the time in the entire districit,therefor we could not call it Reiger Park,we did not only serve Reiger Park.

The other schools was not bulid yet,all these other schools we have now (Geluksdal Secondary,Eden Park Secondary,etc) they came after Apartheid and when Apartheid ended all the model C also opened up after Apartheid, so many of the learners could also attend those schools on the eastrand but before all of the other schools Oosrand was the only school in the entire region.The government came up with the desion of the school reason being,there was no highschool on the East Rand,learners had to travel by train to William highschool in Benoni. The school was designed for all population groups although it was Apartheid the people still had to go schools.We have had group areas act,set at different race groups.Reiger Park was designated as a coloured area in 1964(august), so they needed a highschool for coloured people.

NELSON MANDELA'S VISIT

On the 26th of June 1995 the first black president of South Africa visited Oosrand Secondary school.
